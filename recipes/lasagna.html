Lasagna

A delightful Italian classic, perfect for a cozy family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Sheet
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta sheets in water until al dente.
  2. Layer Sheets in Pan
  3. Alternating between pasta, sauce, meat, and cheese, fill up the lasagna pan
  4. Bake at 350F for 40 minutes
